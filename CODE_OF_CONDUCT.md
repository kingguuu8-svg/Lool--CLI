# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, practical, and generous with context.

We want PocketCLI to be a welcoming project for:

- students
- hobbyists
- developers shipping self-hosted tools
- people exploring terminal-first AI workflows

## Expected behavior

- assume good intent
- focus on technical clarity, not status
- give actionable feedback
- keep discussions constructive and on topic
- respect privacy and security concerns

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment or personal attacks
- dismissive or insulting language
- publishing private credentials or exploit details irresponsibly
- derailing issues and pull requests

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that harm the project environment.
